AKAN NAME GENERATOR

Akan names come from a traditional naming system for people from countries such as Ivory Coast, Togo and Ghana. A child name is determined by factors surrounding his or her birth.The day of their birth or the manner in which they were born may determine which name they get irrespective of their gender.These Akan names signify the day one is born. According to Akan tradition, each child is born with a name associated to their soul. The day the child is born is therefore determined by the soul.
